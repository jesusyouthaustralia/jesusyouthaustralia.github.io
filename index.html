A Conference Beyond the Ordinary

The Jesus Youth Movement understands the new evangelisation is the first priority for the Church. In one sense, our mission is simple and clear: to propose once again to young people the entire Gospel of salvation in Jesus Christ.

In this context, Jesus Youth Australia decided to organise an annual Youth Conference that helps young people to encounter Jesus in a life changing way. Conference will encourage young people to take bold commitment to live as missionary disciples of Jesus Christ.

With a lineup of amazing speakers, life changing sessions, eucharistic celebrations, awesome workshops, live theatre and music performances, the CHOSEN conference is the perfect opportunity for you to rediscover GOD and His purpose for your life!

KEYNOTE TALKS

Powerful talks from Bishops and inspiring sessions from well known speakers.

ON-STAGE DISCUSSIONS

Live Discussions and open question and answer sessions with the speakers.

MUSIC CONCERTS

This is where we sit down, grab a cup of coffee and dial in the details.

THEATER PRESENTATIONS

Stunning live theater productions by professionals that will move your heart.

Powerful Workshops

Interactive workshops exploring key topics of our faith at a deeper level.

Rallies

Intense worship session combining prayer, awsome music, talks and drama.

Fr Rob Galea

FRG Ministries

Fr Rob Galea is an ordained Catholic Priest and is currently serving in Sandhurst Diocese, Victoria. He is a singer and songwriter with an international fan base. Mark Nimo

Faith Formation

Mark earned a Masters in Pastoral Studies from Catholic Theological Union and an Ecumenical Doctor of Ministry. Director of Faith Formation at St. Columbanus Church and Augustus Tolton Catholic Academy in the Archdiocese of Chicago, he has ministered in over forty countries worldwide.

Bp. Mark Edwards OMI

The 'Chosen' conference was very impressive. It took those attending on a journey with multiple opportunities to encounter Jesus. I was there for a morning session where those attending broke into groups of three and the two prayed over/for the other one. And then rotate. I thought it powerful. At one stage, also, we prayed for those trying to work out their vocation. In turn we prayed for those who were trying to discern their profession, whether/who they should marry, and those thinking of religious life or priesthood. Each group stood as we prayed for them. About 10 stood when we were praying for those discerning religious life/priesthood.

Fr Chris Eaton, MGL

It was an absolute plessure to be part of the first Jesus Youth Chosen Conference. It was filled with so much joy and life. The event itself was well organised and soaked in prayer. I enjoyed being aroung young people who were alive and ready to dance and sing their hearts out. I loved the opportunity to rap and bring the Gospel to life in a new way. Thank you all the organisers for a very successful conference. If you are a young person wanting to grow deeper in your faith and be surrounded by like minded faith filled people, Chosen 2019 is the place to be.
